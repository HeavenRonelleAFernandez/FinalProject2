What is it about?

Poverty, by definition, is not having enough money to meet basic needs including food, clothing and shelter. It is present almost everywhere in the world and it is a world crisis that is hard to combat considering the amount of people walking in this planet. In the context of the first Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), this website supports this, giving context and presenting solutions with respect to this goal.

Who is it about?

Society

Poverty is a global crisis. Third-world countries suffer the most in this area and even first-world countries have this problem in parts of their society.

Poverty is present in the Philippines. There are many rural areas in the country and only some provinces or regions don't have this issue to be worried about.

Poverty can be present in society, depending on the conditions. Places like Davao City have regions that do suffer poverty but for the most part, it doesn't have this problem. This is because Davao is more developed as a place in the country compared to other areas.

What is this for?

This website is made to spread awareness about the presence of poverty in different places of the world. Some people may be unaware since many people are spoiled with the riches of their area, not knowing the conditions outside their area. This website is made to influence people to think differently and motivate them to do something to combat this issue in any way possible.
